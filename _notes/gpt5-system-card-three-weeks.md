Three weeks of reading and re-reading the card, with a clinical-deployment lens. A few observations.

## What's genuinely useful

The expanded refusal taxonomy is the most clinically relevant addition, and it is better than I expected.

## What's missing

There is still very little about sustained multi-session interaction, which is exactly the regime where the cases I see clinically arise.

## What the stress tests actually tell us

Stress-test pass rates are reassuring at the margin and uninformative at the center.
