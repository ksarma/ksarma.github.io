After a year of advisory conversations, these five questions do most of the work of telling me whether a team has thought seriously about clinical safety.

1. What is the worst thing your product can do to a user, and how would you find out it happened?
2. Who on your team can overrule the growth metric?
3. What is your plan for the user who is in crisis right now?
4. How do you know your model hasn't drifted since you last validated it?
5. What would make you pull the product?

The companies that have good answers are not always the ones with the best technology. But they are the ones I worry about least.
